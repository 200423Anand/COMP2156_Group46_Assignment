The full form of SDLC is Software Development Life Cycle.

The Software Development Life Cycle is a systematic process for building software that ensures the quality and correctness of the final product.
The key stages of SDLC is Planning,Requirements Gathering and Analysis,Design, Testing, Deployment, Maintenance.
