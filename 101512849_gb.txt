George Brown College (GBC) is a public college in Toronto, Canada, known for its hands-on learning and 
industry-focused programs. It offers diplomas, degrees, and certificates across various fields such as 
business, technology, health sciences, and hospitality.

Key Highlights:
- Campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
- Programs: Over 160+ full-time and 200+ continuing education programs.
- Industry Connections: Strong partnerships with businesses for co-ops and internships.
- Diversity: Welcomes students from 100+ countries.
- Rankings: Recognized as a leading college in applied research and innovation.

Motto: "Hands-on learning, real-world experience!"
